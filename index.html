Ritser Privacy Toolkit - Protecting free speech

Many people living in authoritarian countries aren't aware of the tools available to circumvent censorship and surveillance. And when they are aware of these tools, it's difficult to download them due to censorship. This prevents important discussion of political ideas and protests from occurring.

To help empower the people in such situations, we've created a package consisting of a guide to internet privacy and a copy of the Tor browser. Redistributing this package is encouraged, as this web page may end up being blocked in the future.
